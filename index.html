What is a SPA: Single-Page Application?

Revolution! This is the word that defines how much the front-end has grown in recent years and the SPA (Single-Page Application) is the culmination of that.

The SPA application can be built in different ways, the most common being being assisted by a Javascript framework like Angular or Vue.js, both have route systems.
